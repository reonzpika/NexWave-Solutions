# Marketing & Customer Acquisition Strategy Template

## Purpose of This Stage/Scenario
Develop and execute marketing strategies to acquire customers, build brand awareness, and create sustainable growth channels. This stage covers positioning, go-to-market plans, and customer acquisition tactics.

## Key Questions for Founders
- Who is your target customer?
- What's your brand positioning and messaging?
- What channels will you use to reach customers?
- What's your customer acquisition cost (CAC) target?
- How will you differentiate from competitors?
- What content will you create?
- What's your sales process and funnel?

## Common Documents to Prepare
- Brand positioning document
- Go-to-market plan
- Marketing channel strategy
- Content calendar
- Sales process documentation
- Customer acquisition plan
- PR and communications plan
- Marketing metrics dashboard

## Typical Milestones and Checkpoints
1. **Brand Positioning** - Define brand and messaging framework
2. **Go-to-Market Plan** - Create comprehensive GTM strategy
3. **Channel Selection** - Choose and prioritize marketing channels
4. **Content Strategy** - Develop content calendar and assets
5. **Sales Process** - Document sales funnel and process
6. **Launch Campaign** - Execute initial marketing campaigns
7. **Optimization** - Measure, analyze, and optimize performance

## Pitfalls to Avoid
- Unclear positioning: Vague or generic brand messaging
- Channel overload: Trying too many channels at once
- Ignoring CAC: Not tracking customer acquisition costs
- No content strategy: Inconsistent or random content creation
- Weak sales process: Unclear or undefined sales funnel
- Ignoring competitors: Not differentiating from competition
- No measurement: Not tracking marketing metrics

## Example Prompts for AI to Reference During Setup Conversations
- "Let's define your brand positioning. How do you want to be perceived?"
- "What marketing channels make sense for your target customers?"
- "Let's create your go-to-market plan with specific tactics."
- "What's your content strategy? Let's build a content calendar."
- "How will you acquire customers? Let's map out your sales funnel."
- "What are your key marketing metrics? Let's set up tracking."
