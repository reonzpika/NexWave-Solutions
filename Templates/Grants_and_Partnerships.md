# Grants and Partnership Development Template

## Purpose of This Stage/Scenario
Identify and pursue grant opportunities, develop strategic partnerships, and manage collaboration frameworks. This stage helps secure non-dilutive funding and strategic relationships that accelerate growth.

## Key Questions for Founders
- What types of grants are available for your sector/stage?
- What partnerships would be most valuable?
- What are grant application requirements and deadlines?
- What compliance and reporting obligations exist?
- What's your partnership value proposition?
- How will you manage grant funds and reporting?
- What legal agreements are needed for partnerships?

## Common Documents to Prepare
- Grant opportunity research
- Grant proposals and applications
- Partnership proposals
- Partnership agreements and contracts
- Compliance checklists
- Reporting templates
- Collaboration frameworks
- Due diligence materials

## Typical Milestones and Checkpoints
1. **Grant Research** - Identify relevant grant opportunities
2. **Partnership Strategy** - Define partnership goals and targets
3. **Proposal Development** - Create grant and partnership proposals
4. **Application Submission** - Submit grant applications on time
5. **Partnership Negotiation** - Negotiate partnership terms
6. **Compliance Setup** - Establish compliance and reporting systems
7. **Relationship Management** - Maintain ongoing grant and partnership relationships

## Pitfalls to Avoid
- Missing deadlines: Not tracking grant application deadlines
- Unclear proposals: Vague or unfocused grant/partnership proposals
- Compliance gaps: Not understanding reporting requirements
- Wrong fit: Pursuing grants/partnerships that don't align with goals
- Poor relationship management: Not maintaining grant/partnership relationships
- No tracking system: Losing track of applications and agreements
- Underestimating time: Grant applications take more time than expected
